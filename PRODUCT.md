# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People tracking receipts and invoices from a phone, often right after a purchase or while preparing expenses for accounting. They need quick capture, clear review, and dependable local storage without a server account.

## Product Purpose

Spend Manager helps users scan or manually enter expenses, extract receipt details with Gemini when configured, review the result, store records locally, and export summaries for accounting.

## Brand Personality

Practical, calm, trustworthy. The interface should feel like a focused mobile utility: familiar controls, readable forms, clear feedback, and no decorative friction.

## Anti-references

Avoid desktop-first layouts squeezed into a phone viewport, horizontal scrolling, cramped form controls, hidden core actions on mobile, decorative dashboards, and heavy visual effects that slow down expense entry.

## Design Principles

- Mobile-first reliability: every primary workflow must fit and work on small phones.
- Review before save: AI output should always remain editable and easy to verify.
- Local-first trust: make storage and API-key handling clear without adding anxiety.
- Accounting clarity: summaries, currencies, categories, and exports should be scannable.
- Familiar controls: use native-feeling form, navigation, and action patterns over novelty.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls, keep touch targets at least 44px where possible, support reduced motion, preserve keyboard focus states, and never require horizontal scrolling on mobile.
